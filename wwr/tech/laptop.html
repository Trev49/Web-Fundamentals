Standard Laptops

These types of laptops are also really common and you likely grew up with these as they have been around for decades. They are what you expect for the most part but there are lots of different features that come depending on the model that you have. You will likely see these kind of devices as seen in these images.

Pros:

  • There are many options and add ons that you can decide on based on your budget.
  • You can often find a standard laptop between $300 to $500 range and they come with a variety of ports to allow connections to any external equipment as needed.
  • As mentioned earlier the lower price range makes it more affordable for college students and they will be able to accomplish most if not all things that a college student needs to do.
  • The bigger size allows for better cooling allowing some more higher power software.

Cons:

  • While they are really useful and affordable, they can become a little bulky to carry around making it less portable compared to other options out there.
  • Standard Laptops vary greatly when it comes to durability. There have been reports of people having issues after a year. Some others will last years. You really never know how long they will last.
  • Battery quality often wears out quickly requiring bringing power cords.

Rundown:

Standard Laptops are great options for those who are just looking for something simple and easy to use without advanced new technology. Their huge variety allows you to find the one that works best for you. Even with the varied reliability, many people use them for everyday studies and has worked for many years. This type of device is recommended for people who are beginners to computing as well.
